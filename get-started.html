Get Started with Aurum

Follow these simple steps to begin your journey with Aurum.

Welcome to Aurum

This guide will walk you through everything you need to know to get started on the platform.

Quick Start Guide

Getting started with Aurum takes just a few minutes. Whether you're here to explore investment opportunities or to participate in the partner program, this guide covers everything you need.

Follow the steps below in order to ensure a smooth onboarding experience.

Onboarding Steps

Create Your Account

Click the "Sign Up" button and provide your basic information including email address and a secure password. Make sure to use a strong password that you don't use elsewhere.

Tip: Use a password manager to generate and store a secure password.

Verify Your Email

Check your email inbox for a verification message from Aurum. Click the verification link to confirm your email address. This step is required for account security.

Note: If you don't see the email, check your spam folder.

Complete Your Profile

Log in to your new account and complete your profile information. This includes personal details, contact information, and any required verification documents.

Important: Accurate information ensures smooth account operations and compliance.

Review Terms and Conditions

Carefully read through the terms of service, privacy policy, and any other legal documents. Understanding these terms is essential before using the platform.

Reminder: You must agree to the terms to use Aurum services.

Set Up Security Features

Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) for additional account security. Configure your security preferences and recovery options in case you need to regain access to your account.

Best Practice: Always enable 2FA to protect your account from unauthorized access.

Explore the Dashboard

Once your account is set up, take time to familiarize yourself with the dashboard. Explore available features, read educational resources, and understand how to navigate the platform.

Get Your Referral Link

If you're interested in the partner program, navigate to the referral settings to obtain your unique referral link. You can start sharing this link with your network right away.

Go to Referral Settings

Start Using Aurum

You're all set! Begin exploring opportunities, participating in programs, or sharing your referral link. If you have questions, consult the help center or contact support.

Account Requirements

Age Requirement

You must be at least 18 years old (or the age of majority in your jurisdiction) to create an account.

Valid Email

A working email address is required for account verification and important communications.

Identity Verification

Depending on your activities, you may need to complete identity verification with supporting documents.

Jurisdictional Eligibility

Aurum services may not be available in all countries. Verify that your location is supported.

Compliance

You must agree to comply with all applicable laws and Aurum's terms of service.

Honest Information

All information provided must be accurate, complete, and truthful.

What to Expect

Your Journey with Aurum

After completing the onboarding process, you'll have access to:

  • A personalized dashboard showing your account overview
  • Access to available investment opportunities and programs
  • Educational resources to help you make informed decisions
  • Tools for tracking your activity and performance
  • Your unique referral link if participating in the partner program
  • Customer support channels for assistance

Important Reminders

Security First

Never share your password or 2FA codes with anyone, including Aurum support staff. Aurum will never ask for this information.

Be Informed

Take time to understand any financial products or opportunities before participating. Consider seeking independent financial advice if needed.

Stay Updated

Keep your contact information current and monitor your email for important updates from Aurum regarding your account or the platform.
