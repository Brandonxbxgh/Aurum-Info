Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to refer people to use Aurum?

No. The referral program is completely optional. You can use all Aurum bots and features without ever referring anyone.

How do I get paid?

Referral commissions are automatically credited to your account balance and can be withdrawn at any time, subject to minimum withdrawal requirements.

Is there a limit to how much I can earn?

No, there is no limit. The more active traders you refer, the more you can earn. Some top referrers earn substantial passive income through the program.

Can my referrals also refer others?

Yes! Your referrals can create their own referral networks, and you'll earn second-tier commissions from their referrals as well.

How are commissions calculated?

Commissions are calculated as a percentage of the trading fees generated by your referrals. They are credited in real-time as trades are executed.
