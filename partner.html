Partner Program

Share Aurum with others and earn rewards for successful referrals.

About the Program

The Aurum Partner Program is designed to reward community members who help grow our platform by referring new users.

How It Works

As a partner, you receive a unique referral link that you can share with friends, family, or your network. When someone signs up using your link and meets the qualifying criteria, you earn rewards.

The program is designed to be straightforward and transparent, with clear terms and no hidden conditions.

Partner Benefits

Earn Rewards

Receive compensation for each successful referral that meets the program requirements.

Track Performance

Monitor your referrals and earnings through a dedicated dashboard.

No Limits

There's no cap on how many people you can refer or how much you can earn.

Easy Sharing

Use your unique link across social media, email, or any communication channel.

Support Resources

Access marketing materials and guidance to help you succeed as a partner.

Community Recognition

Top performers may receive special recognition and additional benefits.

Eligibility & Requirements

Who Can Become a Partner?

  • Must be an active Aurum user in good standing
  • Must be of legal age in your jurisdiction
  • Must comply with all applicable laws and regulations
  • Must use ethical marketing practices when sharing your link

Partners are expected to represent Aurum accurately and professionally. Misrepresentation or spam behavior may result in program removal.

Getting Started as a Partner

Step 1: Sign Up

Create your Aurum account if you haven't already. Ensure your profile is complete and verified.

Step 2: Get Your Referral Link

Once your account is active, you'll receive a unique referral link. You can manage this link in your referral settings.

Step 3: Share Your Link

Distribute your referral link through your preferred channels - social media, email, blogs, or direct messaging.

Step 4: Track Your Referrals

Monitor your performance and earnings through your partner dashboard. You'll see when someone signs up using your link.

Step 5: Receive Rewards

Once your referrals meet the qualifying criteria, your rewards will be processed according to the program terms.

Best Practices for Partners

Be Authentic

Share genuine experiences and honest perspectives about Aurum. Authenticity builds trust.

Provide Value

Focus on how Aurum can benefit your audience rather than just promoting for rewards.

Stay Compliant

Always follow applicable laws regarding endorsements and disclosures in your jurisdiction.

Respect Privacy

Never spam or share your link in unwelcome contexts. Respect others' preferences.

Be Accurate

Ensure all information you share about Aurum is accurate and up-to-date.

Build Relationships

Focus on building genuine connections rather than just acquiring referrals.

Program Terms

The Aurum Partner Program is subject to terms and conditions that may be updated from time to time. Key points include:

  • Rewards are earned only when referred users meet specific qualifying criteria
  • Aurum reserves the right to modify or terminate the program at any time
  • Partners found violating terms may be removed from the program
  • All referral activities must comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • Partners are independent and not employees or representatives of Aurum

For complete terms and conditions, please review the partner agreement provided upon enrollment.
